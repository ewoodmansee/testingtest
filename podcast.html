YOUR PODCAST NAME

A meme idea I stole from Twitter in an attempt to come up with a webpage so I can practice my HTML.

I'd link to the actual image but I'd feel weird dropping a link to Twitter on this.

The idea is quite simple, though. You use your last text + one of the following prompts to come up with your podcast name. This is actually one of the less interesting memes, but sometimes you just need an idea.

YOUR LAST TEXT + ...

  • JANUARY: A slice of American Life.

  • FEBRUARY: A Six Part Series.

  • MARCH: Like You've Never Heard It Before!

  • APRIL: A Brilliant New Perspective.

  • MAY: A Deep Dive into the Human Brain.

  • JUNE: And Other Stuff You Should Know.

  • JULY: A Message from Extraterrestials?

  • AUGUST: A Critical Look at the English Language.

  • SEPTEMBER: A New Theory of Relativity.

  • OCTOBER: History's Best Kept Secret?

  • NOVEMBER: Unpacking...the Truth.

  • DECEMBER: A True Crime Series.
